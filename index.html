Identity & Access Management

A presentation by a Security Solutions Lead

What is IAM?

Identity and Access Management (IAM) is the security discipline that makes it possible for the right entities to use the right resources when they need to, without interference, using the devices they want to use.

My Role as a Security Solutions Lead

  • Defining IAM Strategy
  • Leading IAM Projects
  • Evaluating and Implementing IAM Solutions
  • Ensuring Compliance

Key IAM Concepts

  • Authentication
  • Authorization
  • Single Sign-On (SSO)
  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
  • Privileged Access Management (PAM)
